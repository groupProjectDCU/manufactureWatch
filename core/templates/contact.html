This system helps ACME Manufacturing Corp. to track the operational status, repair requests, and history of factory machinery, ensuring smooth operation and timely repairs.

Technicians can create a fault report by selecting a machine, providing the details of the issue, and submitting a new "Fault" case. You can add images and notes about the fault for better clarity.

Managers and technicians can view the status of machines through the dashboard. They can also filter or search machines by status, collection, or repair case.
