About Factory Machinery Status & Repair Tracking System

A comprehensive solution to track machinery status, repairs, and maintenance at ACME Manufacturing Corp.

Purpose of the System

The Factory Machinery Status & Repair Tracking System was developed to help ACME Manufacturing Corp. effectively monitor the health and operational status of its machinery. The system allows for quick tracking of faults, repairs, and statuses for all factory machinery, ensuring high productivity, reducing downtime, and improving overall operational efficiency.

Key Features

Real-Time Status Updates

The system tracks the real-time status of all machinery, including "OK," "Warning," and "Fault" statuses.

Repair and Fault Management

Technicians and repair teams can log faults and track repair progress with notes, images, and unique case IDs.

Manager Dashboard

Managers can view a comprehensive dashboard, create reports, and manage user roles, including assigning technicians to machinery.

How the System Works

1. Machines are monitored continuously, and statuses are updated in real-time.

2. When a fault is detected, a technician logs the issue, and a unique fault case is created.

3. The repair team works on the case, adding updates and marking the fault as resolved once fixed.

4. Managers oversee the entire process, generate reports, and manage machine and technician assignments.
