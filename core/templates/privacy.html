Privacy Policy

Protecting your data at ACME Manufacturing Corp.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At ACME Manufacturing Corp., we value the privacy of our employees, technicians, repair personnel, and managers who use the Factory Machinery Status & Repair Tracking System. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • User Account Information: Name, email address, and role assignment.
  • Machine Interaction Data: Notes, images, and comments submitted for fault cases or warnings.
  • Usage Data: Login times and pages visited for security and performance monitoring.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide role-based access to the system.
  • Track machinery statuses and repairs.
  • Generate reports and improve user experience.

Data Security

We employ encryption and secure authentication to safeguard your data, accessible only to authorized personnel.

Third-Party Sharing

Data is not shared externally except via the anonymized API for machine statuses or as required by law.

Your Rights

Contact acme@gmail.com to access or delete your data.
